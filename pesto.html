Pasta

Description

When ramps are in reason, try making this tasty, easy ramp pesto. Tastes great with pasta or on bread.

Ingredients

  • Ramps leaves
  • Pine nuts
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Combine ramp leaves, pine nuts, and garlic in a food processor. Process until finely chopped. With the food processor running, add olive oil and process everything until smooth. Transfer mixture to a bowl and mix in grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt to taste.
