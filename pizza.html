Pizza

Description

Pizza is an italian dish, made of primarily tomato sauce, bread and cheese. A variety of toppings can be added to it such a vegetables like capsicum, onions, Mushrooms and olives as well as meats such as chicken or pepperoni.

Once the base is assembled with the toppings and other ingredients, it is baked. This is traditionally done in a woodfire oven, but modern microwaves will do just fine as well.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Puree
    • Tomatoes
  • 100g Cheese
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 2 Onions
  • 50g Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Slather the bread with the puree, leaving only the crust untouched
  2. Grate the cheese and sprinkle half of it evenly all over the bread
  3. Wash and cut the vegetables, then put them all over the bread
  4. Carefully put the bread into the oven, and allow it to cook for around 8-12 minutes
  5. Take the bread out of the oven and cut it into slices depending on it's sizze
